What is Phishing?

Phishing is when criminals pretend to be a trusted company or person to trick you into giving them your personal information, passwords, or money.

Common Phishing Tricks:

  • Fake emails that look like they're from your bank
  • Text messages claiming your package needs redelivery
  • Websites that look real but have slightly wrong addresses
  • Urgent messages saying your account will be closed
  • Requests for passwords or credit card numbers

These scams are designed to create panic and rush you into clicking dangerous links without thinking. That's where we come in.

Why Scammers Target Seniors

Unfortunately, seniors are often targeted by online scammers because:

  • Trust: Seniors tend to be more trusting and polite
  • Savings: Scammers know seniors may have retirement savings
  • Less Familiarity: The internet changes fast, making it hard to spot new tricks
  • Isolation: Seniors living alone may not have someone to ask for help

You are not alone. Millions of people fall for these scams every year. Silver Umbrella is here to help you stay one step ahead.
